About us

Established in the year 2017, Blossom English Academy is committed to elevate the English communication and written ability of students and professionals. The academy is located in Manchester of Tamilnadu, Coimbatore. It is headed by Mr L.Krishnaraj, an eminent academician in English with a post master degree and a certified trainer of various examinations. He has also got a decade of experience English language teaching and training. The director produced many successful candidates in BEC, IELTS and TOEFL. English is required to every aspirant of a good position in the job market. The academy offers multidimensional and customized training to meet your career goals. The academy also trains schools and college students in writing and speaking in order to enrich their communication and excel their performance. The academy ensures that it keeps in Pace with society. Hence it has made an attempt to digitalize the training. We have started offering online training for this year due to global pandemic covid 19
